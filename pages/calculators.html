Investment Calculators

Eight purpose-built calculators to model cashflows, evaluate yields, and stress-test your hard-asset portfolio — completely free.

Every serious investor needs sharp tools. These calculators are designed specifically for the sectors we cover — shipping, pipelines, mining, energy, and high-yield income. No sign-up required. Run your numbers, test your assumptions, and make better decisions.

Shipping Cashflow Calculator

Model daily revenue, operating expenses, and net cashflow for tanker, bulk carrier, and LNG fleets. Input TCE rates, OPEX, and debt service to see how different rate environments affect distributable cashflow per share.

Dividend Yield Calculator

Calculate current yield, yield on cost, and forward yield based on projected dividend growth. Compare annualized income across positions and see how reinvestment compounds your yield over 5, 10, and 20-year horizons.

Pipeline DCF Valuation

Run a discounted cashflow analysis tailored for midstream MLPs and pipeline corporations. Factor in contracted revenue, volume growth, maintenance capex, and distribution coverage ratios to estimate intrinsic value.

Mining Break-Even Analyzer

Determine the commodity price at which a mining operation turns profitable. Input all-in sustaining costs (AISC), production volume, royalties, and corporate overhead to find the true break-even price per ounce or ton.

DRIP Compounding Simulator

Visualize the power of dividend reinvestment over time. Enter your starting position, dividend yield, payout frequency, and expected growth rate to see how reinvested distributions build your share count and income stream exponentially.

Portfolio Income Planner

Plan your annual dividend income across all holdings. Add each position with its yield and payment schedule to build a month-by-month income forecast. Identify gaps in your payment calendar and optimize for steady monthly cashflow.

NAV Discount Calculator

Evaluate whether a BDC, CEF, or shipping company trades at a premium or discount to its net asset value. Input total assets, liabilities, and shares outstanding to calculate NAV per share and compare it to the current market price.

Commodity Supercycle Tracker

Overlay historical commodity price cycles with current market data. Track oil, natural gas, iron ore, copper, and gold against their long-term averages to gauge where we stand in the current supercycle and identify undervalued entry points.
