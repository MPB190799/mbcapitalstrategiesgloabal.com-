Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

MB Capital Strategies Global is an independent research and education platform focused on dividend investing in hard assets — specifically energy, shipping, mining, and pipeline sectors. We provide clear analysis, free tools, and transparent portfolio tracking to help investors make informed decisions.

Hard assets are physical, tangible assets that have intrinsic value — things like oil reserves, ships, mines, and pipelines. Unlike software companies valued on growth expectations, hard asset companies generate cashflows from real, physical infrastructure. They tend to pay higher dividends because their business models are based on extracting and transporting real commodities.

We focus on six core sectors: Shipping (tankers, bulk carriers, LNG), Mining (gold, copper, coal), Pipelines & Midstream (MLPs, infrastructure), Energy & Upstream (oil, gas), High-Yield & BDCs (business development companies, REITs), and Commodity Supercycles (uranium, zinc, nickel, copper).

Our 80/20 approach allocates 80% of the portfolio to stable, high-dividend payers from pipelines, mining royalties, and infrastructure — assets that produce consistent cashflows. The remaining 20% is held in ultrashort bonds, providing liquidity for tactical opportunities when markets pull back. This balance maximizes income while maintaining flexibility.
